Summer Internship

Summary

Tasks

  • During my internship with the National Ocean Service in Silver Spring, MD, from May 2023 to August 2023, I had the opportunity to work on the Great Lakes Historical Levels project, where I played a pivotal role in utilizing Python for data visualization. One of my primary responsibilities involved leading this project, where I used Python, particularly Matplotlib and Plotly, to create interactive graphs that effectively visualized historical data. These visualizations allowed us to discern meaningful connections to the glacial recession, shedding light on the complex geological processes impacting the Great Lakes.
  • Additionally, I had the privilege of developing an ESRI GIS Story Map, which served as an innovative and dynamic platform for presenting our findings. These data visualization products not only showcased our insights into the historical water levels but also offered a comprehensive understanding of the geological events' profound implications on the Great Lakes communities.
  • Throughout the internship, collaboration played a significant role in our success. I worked closely with project mentors, technical experts, and communication teams, fostering a multidisciplinary approach to our work. This collaborative environment allowed us to ensure effective documentation and the widespread dissemination of critical water level data and our research findings. This, in turn, contributed to a broader community understanding of water level trends and their far-reaching implications.
  • Moreover, as a culmination of my internship journey, I had the honor of presenting our research findings. This presentation marked a significant milestone in my internship experience and provided me with an opportunity to share our valuable insights with a broader audience. Notably, I am proud to mention that, as a recognition of my dedication and contributions during the internship, I earned an award.
  • My internship experience was not only a professional growth opportunity but also a chance to make a meaningful impact through data analysis and visualization, as well as to share our findings with others in the field. It was a rewarding journey that culminated in an award, underlining the significance of the work we accomplished during this enriching experience.
